Careers

Lead Duck Designer:

The Wild and Crazy individual will be a muralist, sculptor, graphic designer, dreamer and doer that will bring our ducking company the innovation to move up in the industry. Salaried

Quality Duck Control:

Attention to detail from every bill to every faux feather. No blemish shall go unseen. Salaried.

Float Engineer:

Creation through inspection, our desired float engineer shall have experience from bath tub to sea. Salaried

Buoyancy Inspector:

Must have the wherewithal to know the difference between a rock and a rubber ducky and which one should float. Salaried

Rubber Inspector:

Attention to detail, understand the malleable nature of the material and have a complete understanding of rubber tension and float ability. Salaried.

Lead Duck Sales:

A reel go-getter who grabs the fish by the tail, who goes out and grabs the goose by the beak and drives sales and follows up with exceptional service. Salary + Commission.
